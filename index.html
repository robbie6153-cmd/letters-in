Letters In – Free Daily Word Game

Play Letters In, a free daily word puzzle game. Find as many words as possible from 9 letters before time runs out. Perfect for fans of Wordle, anagrams, and brain training games.

How to Play Letters In

Letters In is a free daily word game. Each day, players are given 9 letters and must make as many valid words as possible before the timer runs out.

Words must be at least 3 letters long. Longer words score more points, so players are encouraged to find as many high-value words as possible before time runs out.

The game resets daily, giving all players the same challenge each day.

Why Word Games Matter

Word games can help improve vocabulary, spelling, pattern recognition and mental agility.

Daily puzzle games also encourage memory, concentration and problem-solving skills.

Letters In was designed to be simple, accessible and enjoyable while providing a fun mental challenge each day.

How To Play

1. Make as many words as you can from the 9 letters in 200 seconds.

2. Words must be in the English dictionary and three letters or more.

3. You score one point per word, two points for a 6 letter word, three points for a 7 letter word, four points for an 8 letter word and five points for a 9 letter word.

4. There will always be a 9 letter word.

5. No words such as names and places.

6. A new game begins at midnight GMT.
