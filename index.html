How To Play

1. Make as many words as you can from the 9 letters in 200 seconds.

2. Words must be in the English dictionary and three letters or more.

3. You score one point per word, two points for a 6 letter word, three points for a 7 letter word, four points for an 8 letter word and five points for a 9 letter word.

4. There will always be a 9 letter word.

5. No words such as names and places.

6. A new game begins at midnight GMT.
